Tower of Hanoi

Instructions:

Get all 3 disc from smallest (top) to biggest (bottom), in the third column. In other words get the same starting position of discs to the third column.

Visual of win state:

How to play:

Tap the discs to select them, then tap the column you want to move it to.

Rules:

1. You can't stack the bigger discs on smaller discs.

2. You can stack the smaller discs on bigger ones.

3. You can stack as many discs in any column given you are following the rules above
